LSST Detection of Optical Counterparts of Gravitational Waves 2019

May 3-4, 2019
Columbia University, New York City

The workshop will outline the prospects of using LSST to observe the electromagnetic counterparts of gravitational-wave observations by Advanced LIGO, Virgo and KAGRA. We will further discuss what needs to be done in getting ready for these observations before LSST comes online.

Location:
Center for Theoretical Physics, 8th Floor Pupin Hall
Columbia University, New York City
